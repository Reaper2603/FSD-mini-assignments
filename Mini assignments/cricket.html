CRICKET IN INDIA

HOW IT GOT STARTED?

Cricket was introduced to India by British sailors in the 18th century, and the first cricket club was established in 1792. 
India's national cricket team played its first Test match on 25 June 1932 at Lord's, becoming the sixth team to be granted test cricket status
